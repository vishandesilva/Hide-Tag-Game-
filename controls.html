    • Controls

    The controls of the game are quite simple

    • W is used to move up
    • A is used to move left
    • S is used to move down
    • D is used to move right
    • The Aim of the game is to run away from the seeker, as a hider you have 1 power up and 1 trap.
    • The blue icon is a trap, if a hider runs over it, the slow down allowing the seeker to catch him.
    • The orange icon speeds up the hider to help him evade the seeker.

    Lights & Shadows

    A simple game where there are hiders and seekers. The objective is for the seeker to catch all hiders or the hiders evading the seeker till time runs out.
